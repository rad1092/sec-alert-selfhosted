# Roadmap

This roadmap stays narrow on purpose. The first paid release is about a trustworthy self-hosted package, not rapid scope expansion.

## Priority after first customers

- replace placeholder screenshots with real captures
- tighten first-run diagnostics based on buyer feedback
- continue Form 4 reliability hardening where real customer data still exposes gaps
- refine release/update/support wording after legal review

## Explicitly deferred

- hosted SaaS conversion
- teams/accounts
- subscriptions or billing integration
- PostgreSQL support
- higher official watchlist-capacity claims
- broad research-platform scope
- dashboard live polling redesign
- Discord/Telegram expansion
- major scoring-model changes
