# Privacy

## Default posture

This product is self-hosted. By default, the app does not collect telemetry from buyers.

## Data flow

- Your SEC watchlist and filings live in your local runtime.
- Your notification destination settings live in `.env` or local metadata.
- Optional external providers are only used when you configure them.

## External services

If you enable OpenAI, Slack, webhook, or SMTP delivery, the data sent to those services is governed by the settings you provide and the provider's own terms.

## Buyer expectation

Buyers should assume they control the data path because they control the runtime.

## HUMAN LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED

If you plan to sell this commercially, have counsel review whether any privacy notice language needs to change based on your final support and distribution model.
